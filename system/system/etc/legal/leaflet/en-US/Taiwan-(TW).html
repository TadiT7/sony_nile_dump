Low-Power Radio Frequency Device Statement

This device is in compliance with "Administrative Regulations on Low-Power Radio Waves Radiated Devices, NCC, Taiwan."

Article 12

Without permission granted by NCC, any company, enterprise, or user shall not alter the frequency, amplify the transmitting power, or change the features or functions which were originally designed for the certified low-power radio frequency device.

Article 14

The application of the low-power radio frequency device shall not affect aviation safety nor interfere with any legal communication. If an interference is found, the user should cease operating the device until an improvement is made and the interference no longer exists.

The legal communication mentioned above refers to "radio communication" which is operated in compliance with "Telecommunications Act, NCC, Taiwan."

Low-power radio frequency devices must be susceptible to interference from communications or ISM radio wave radiated devices.
